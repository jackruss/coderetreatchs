About

Coderetreat is a day-long, intensive practice event, focusing on the fundamentals of software development and design. By providing developers the opportunity to take part in focused practice, away from the pressures of 'getting things done', the coderetreat format has proven itself to be a highly effective means of skill improvement. Practicing the basic principles of modular and object-oriented design, developers can improve their ability to write code that minimizes the cost of change over time.

Four Rules of DesignMore Info...

  • Pass All Tests
  • Clear, Expressive, and Consistent
  • Duplicates no behavior or configuration
  • Minimal Methods, Classes, and Modules

The Problem: Conway's Game of Life

Each session will focus on coding Conway's Game of Life, while using the four rules of design. After each session, we will have a retrospective chat, then another team and start a new session from scratch. There will be new rules for each new session: See http://coderetreat.org for more details.

Schedule

The coderetreat day consists of 5-6 sessions, each session's learnings building upon previous sessions. The morning focuses on becoming comfortable with the problem domain, breaking old habits and beginning focused self-discovery. The afternoon pushes the envelope by challenging pairs to stretch their skills and understanding of abstractions, modular design and test-driven development.

What is pair programming?

Pair programming is the practice of two developers working together to solve a problem.
